# AGENT2 LOG - Jingdezhen Ceramics Tour Images

## Date: 2026-06-21

## Task
Search for high-quality images suitable for a Jingdezhen ceramics tour route page. The images should have warm, earthy tones (clay, terracotta, cream, dark brown), be high resolution (at least 1200px wide), look artistic and professional (not tourist-snapshot), and preferably show hands-on craft process.

## Search Terms Used

The following search terms were attempted on multiple stock photo platforms:

1. `ceramic workshop studio` -- artisan throwing clay, hands working pottery
2. `pottery kiln` -- traditional Chinese kiln, fire, ceramics firing
3. `porcelain painting` -- blue and white porcelain, hand-painted ceramics
4. `pottery wheel hands` -- close-up of hands shaping clay
5. `clay ceramics art` -- artistic ceramic pieces, glazed pottery
